## Deliverable 1 — Mind Map (`Week_02_Mind_Map.png`)

**Tool:** NotebookLM "Mind Map" feature (or Whimsical / draw.io as fallback)

### Steps
1. In your `CSEP Week 02 — Life Cycle Concepts` notebook, click the **Mind Map** button (or open the Studio panel → Mind Map)
2. Paste the following customisation prompt into the Mind Map chat or customisation field:

```
Create a mind map for Week 02 of my CSEP study — topic: Life Cycle Concepts.

Root node: "Life Cycle Concepts"
First-level branches:
1. Life Cycle Definition & Purpose
2. Six Life Cycle Stages (Concept → Retirement)
3. Decision Gates & Stage Transition Reviews
4. Life Cycle Models (Sequential, Iterative, Concurrent, Evolutionary, Agile SE)
5. Processes vs Stages — The Critical Distinction
6. Cost of Change & Life Cycle Cost (LCC)

Second-level branches: the key definitions and principles from the Topic Summaries,
grouped under the correct first-level branch.

Third-level branches: common exam gotchas and cross-references to ISO 15288 clauses
where relevant (e.g., §6.2 for stages, §6.4 for Technical Processes).

Style: clear, exam-revision oriented, minimal visual clutter, exportable as PNG at
1920×1080 or higher.
```

3. Generate the mind map
4. Export/download as PNG
5. Save as `Week_02_Mind_Map.png` in this folder (`Week_02/`)
6. Tick: - [ ] `Week_02_Mind_Map.png` saved ✅

## Deliverable 2 — Audio Overview (`Week_02_Audio_Overview.m4a`)

**Tool:** NotebookLM "Audio Overview" feature (Studio panel)

### Steps
1. In your `CSEP Week 02 — Life Cycle Concepts` notebook (sources already uploaded above)
2. Open the **Studio** panel → click **Audio Overview** → click **Customize**
3. Paste the following customisation prompt:

```
Produce a deep-dive podcast-style conversation (two hosts) for a CSEP candidate studying
Week 02 on Life Cycle Concepts.

Target audience: an experienced engineer preparing for the INCOSE CSEP exam. Assume they
already know basic engineering but are newly learning formal INCOSE / ISO 15288 terminology.

Cover, in order:
1. Why life cycle concepts matter for the exam and in real projects.
2. The six ISO 15288 life cycle stages — formal definitions verbatim.
3. Each of the following sub-topics in ~3–5 minutes each:
   - Life Cycle Definition & Purpose (system vs project vs product life cycle)
   - The Six Stages (Concept through Retirement)
   - Decision Gates and Stage Transition Reviews
   - Life Cycle Models (Sequential, Iterative, Concurrent, Evolutionary, Agile SE)
   - Processes vs Stages — the critical ISO 15288 distinction
   - Cost of Change & Life Cycle Cost
4. The 3–5 most commonly confused distinctions in this chapter:
   - Stage vs process
   - System vs project vs product life cycle
   - Development stage vs development process
   - Acquisition cost vs life cycle cost
   - ISO 15288 decision gate principles vs named reviews (SRR/PDR/CDR)
5. Three high-yield exam tips specific to this topic.
6. A one-minute recap.

Tone: engaging, technically precise, UK English. Target length: 18–25 minutes.
Do not invent references. Cite SEH5 Ch2 and ISO 15288 §6 where specific claims are made.
```

4. Click **Generate**
5. Listen to a sample to confirm quality
6. Download as `.m4a`
7. Save as `Week_02_Audio_Overview.m4a` in this folder (`Week_02/`)
8. Tick: - [ ] `Week_02_Audio_Overview.m4a` saved ✅

> **If download is unavailable:** Note the NotebookLM share link in `Week_02_References.md` under a new "Generated Multimedia" section, and leave the `.m4a` checkbox unticked.

## Deliverable 3 — Video Overview (`Week_02_Video_Overview.mp4`)

**Tool:** NotebookLM "Video Overview" feature (Studio panel → Video Overview)

### Steps
1. In your `CSEP Week 02 — Life Cycle Concepts` notebook (sources already uploaded)
2. Open the **Studio** panel → click **Video Overview** → click **Customize**
3. Select style: **Explainer** (slides + narration)
4. Select length: **Default** (Life Cycle Concepts has 6 sub-topics — use Default)
5. Paste the following customisation prompt:

```
Produce a narrated explainer video for a CSEP candidate studying Week 02 on Life Cycle Concepts.

Audience: experienced engineer (5+ years) preparing for the INCOSE CSEP exam.

Structure (one slide per beat):
1. Cold open (15–20 sec): state the week topic and why the exam tests it heavily.
2. Learning objectives slide (mirrors Week_02_Study_Plan.md objectives).
3. For each sub-topic, produce 1–3 slides (definition + example + exam watch-out if needed):
   a. Life Cycle Definition & Purpose — system vs project vs product life cycle
   b. Six ISO 15288 Life Cycle Stages — purpose of each stage
   c. Decision Gates — four outcomes; named review vs ISO principle distinction
   d. Life Cycle Models — sequential, iterative, concurrent, evolutionary, Agile SE
   e. Processes vs Stages — the critical distinction; 30 processes / 4 process groups
   f. Cost of Change & LCC — cost curve, Boehm, LCC vs acquisition cost
4. Comparison/distinctions slide: stage vs process; acquisition cost vs LCC;
   system life cycle vs project vs product life cycle.
5. Process/life-cycle diagram slide: the six stages in sequence with decision gates
   (use standard ISO 15288 box-and-arrow style).
6. Exam-tip montage: 3 high-yield exam tips, one per slide.
7. Recap slide: 5 must-remember takeaways.
8. "Next up" slide: Week 03 — Technical Processes Part 1A (Business/Mission Analysis
   → Stakeholder Needs).

Style: UK English narration, measured pace (explainer — not banter).
Cite SEH5 Ch2 section references and ISO 15288 clause numbers as footnotes on slides.
Target runtime: 12–20 minutes.
On-screen text: ≤ 40 words per slide. Narration carries the detail.
```

6. Click **Generate**
7. Once complete, download as `.mp4`
8. Save as `Week_02_Video_Overview.mp4` in this folder (`Week_02/`)
9. Tick: - [ ] `Week_02_Video_Overview.mp4` saved ✅

> **If download is unavailable:** Note the NotebookLM share link in `Week_02_References.md` under a new "Generated Multimedia" section, and leave the `.mp4` checkbox unticked.
